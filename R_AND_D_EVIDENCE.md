# Evidence of Ongoing Research and Product Development

This document serves as formal evidence of ongoing product development and research efforts related to the **Real-Time Transactional Anomaly Detection** platform.

## Ongoing Research Manuscript

A comprehensive research paper detailing the methodology, algorithms (Z-Score, Isolation Forest, One-Class SVM), and preliminary findings is currently in active development for potential submission.

* **Working Title:** Ensemble Machine Learning Approaches for Real-Time Transactional Anomaly Detection
* **Status:** Under Development / Manuscript Preparation
* **Manuscript Link:** [RESEARCH_PAPER_MANUSCRIPT.md](./RESEARCH_PAPER_MANUSCRIPT.md)

## Product Development Status

The anomaly detection platform is actively being developed as a scalable, full-stack product. 

* **Backend Architecture:** A robust FastAPI engine that handles high-velocity simulated transactional data, manages state for live machine learning models, and streams data seamlessly via WebSockets.
* **Frontend Visualization:** A high-performance React application utilizing Tailwind CSS and Recharts to provide a dynamic, dark-themed operations dashboard with real-time explainable AI (XAI) metrics.
* **Current Phase:** Active Local Deployment & Prototype Iteration.
